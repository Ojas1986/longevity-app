The Mission
The Longevity Panel brings together 62 of the world's foremost health and longevity specialists across 26 disciplines — from anti-aging biology to breathwork, heart disease to oral microbiome science, trauma to intermittent fasting.
The goal is to make evidence-based health knowledge accessible, personalised, and actionable — giving everyone access to the same level of expert guidance previously available only to those who could afford a team of personal physicians.
